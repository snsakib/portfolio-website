I never thought of becoming a software developer!

I got myself admitted to "Electrical & Electronic Engineering" as it was my favorite subject.

Everything was working great until I got stuck in one of my university projects where I had to do some coding.

Since I never wrote a single line of code before, I reluctantly began to study programming.

A few days later, I discovered myself solving programming problems by staying up late at night!

There were times when I went to sleep pondering over a problem; I woke up in the middle of the night & realized I've found the solution!

Day by day my curiosity grew exponentially. The more I explored the realm of programming, the more I wanted to discover.

However, it was getting difficult for me to study my university courses and programming at the same time. I had to focus on one thing and leave another.Though It was very tough to decide, I preferred putting my focus on studying programming.

As a result, I started to skip classes; I failed at examinations; my GPA plummeted. It took me 2 years more to complete my graduation! But I never regretted my decision.

During those days, I gained proficiency in various aspects of full-stack web development such as

  • Single Page Applications (SPA)
  • Responsive Web Design
  • MEAN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, Node.js)

I'm a huge admirer of online education. I grasp every opportunity to widen the scope of my knowledge. I've completed numerous courses from various e-learning platforms.

My method of understanding a new technology is "Learn ➜ Break ➜ Fix ➜ Evolve"

To put my learning into practice, I've developed some projects.

Explore my projects & the codebases from my GitHub profile.

I'm kinda mixtrovert & always open to constructive criticism.

During my spare time, I read books, watch documentary films or play chess at Chess.com.
